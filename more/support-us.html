NetLogo is a multi-agent programmable modeling environment. It is used by many hundreds of thousands of students, teachers, and researchers worldwide. It is authored by Uri Wilensky and developed at the CCL. You can use it free of charge. Currently, we are moving towards building an open-source ecosystem to provide for sustainable development of the software.
