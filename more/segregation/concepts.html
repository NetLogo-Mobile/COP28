This model showcases a very important complex systems concepts: a reinforcing feedback loop. Everyone in the model is fine with 5 of 8 neighbors different from them. Despite this, some number randomly start off with fewer than 3 similar neighbors. So, they move. This causes a “ripple effect” whereby a few of their old neighbors drop below the threshold of 3 similar neighbors. So they move. This continues until the population is very segregated, despite everyone being fairly tolerant.
